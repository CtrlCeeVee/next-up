## 2. Admin/Organizer Stories (MVP)

**As an admin, I want to:**

1. **Manage the league night flow**

   * Oversee all matches and players present.
   * Move players between courts or matches as needed.
   * Mark players as no-show or late.

2. **Override automated match assignments**

   * Force matches for fairness or flow control.
   * Adjust scores if necessary.

3. **Participate as a player**

   * Admins can also play while maintaining management privileges.

4. **Maintain historical league data**

   * Persist player stats over multiple league nights.
   * Use historical data for insights and reporting.

---

## 3. League Stories (MVP)

**As a league, I want to:**

1. **Have a fixed schedule of nights**

   * e.g., Monday & Wednesday weekly.
   * Rarely changeable in case of exceptional circumstances.

2. **Allow dynamic player participation**

   * Anyone can join at any time.
   * Ensure matches are dynamically generated based on attendance.

3. **Track persistent stats**

   * Player stats persist indefinitely for long-term tracking.
   * Enable future features like matchmaking and tournament seeding.

---

## 4. Future Features / Scope (Post-MVP)

**Player-focused:**

* Auto-assign solo players to teams.
* Notifications when a match is ready (push/queue).
* Ability to select opponents if agreed.

**Admin-focused:**

* Advanced tournament management (round robin, knockout, seeding).
* Stats dashboards with analytics and heatmaps.

**League-focused:**

* Fairness weighting to reduce unbalanced matches.
* Queue numbering and real-time live schedules.
* Monetization: subscription management, premium analytics.
